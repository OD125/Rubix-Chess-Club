WORLD CHESS EVENTS

Ongoing & Upcomimg

Chess World Cup (Ongoing)

The FIDE World Cup refers to three different events over the years. Since 2005, it has been a 128-player single-elimination chess tournament, forming part of the qualification for the World Chess Championship.

Biel Chess Festival (Ongoing)

The Biel International Chess Festival is an annual chess tournament that takes place in Biel/Bienne, Switzerland.

Norway Chess (Upcomimg)

Norway Chess is an annual closed chess tournament, typically taking place in the May to June time period every year.

World Chess Championship (Upcomimg)

The World Chess Championship is played to determine the world champion in chess. The current world champion is Magnus Carlsen of Norway.
